Tenses

To Be Tenses

Present: is/am/are → I am in the class.

Past: was/were → I was a doctor.

Future: shall be/will be → I shall be a doctor.

Simple Tenses

Present: do/does → I play a game.

Past: did → I played a game.

Future: shall/will → I shall play a game.

Continuous Tenses

Present: is/am/are → I am playing.

Past: was/were → I was eating.

Future: shall be/will be → They will be eating.

Perfect Tenses

Present: has/have → I have eaten a meal.

Past: had → I had brought a book.

Future: shall have/will have → I shall have drunk milk.
